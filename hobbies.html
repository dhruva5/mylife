Dhruva's Favorite Hobbies

Running

Running is one of my favorite hobbies since I did track in 7th and 8th grade.
I love the tension you get right before a race starts and I love the pain
you feel after a hard workout. Running makes me be more focused and it helps me stay relaxed.

Watching TV

Watching TV after a long day is the best thing in the world.
My favorite shows are Big Bang Theory, Grey's Anantomy, and The Flash.
I usually watch TV as soon as I get home from school to clear
my mind from all the chaos. It's a great way to de-stress your mind after a long day of work.

Reading

Reading is the best way to get lost in your thoughts.
It not only is good for you on an academic level, however it causes you think more
creative. It makes your thoughts grow and help
you interpret images/concepts better. It is peaceful yet wild at the same time.
